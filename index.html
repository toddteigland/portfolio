A Little About Me

Embarking on a transformative journey from set dresser to web developer, I've always been fueled by the desire to solve problems collaboratively and effectively. My coding expedition commenced with mastering the intricacies of HTML and CSS at CodeAcademy in 2020. Progressively immersing myself into the dynamic world of JavaScript the following year, my passion was ignited further, leading me to complete a full-stack web development diploma at Lighthouse Labs by 2023. My passion has been based around crafting dynamic and interactive websites, ensuring stellar user experiences and high traffic, with notable projects like On the Run, MoonShot, Kanuck Auto, and Tweeter under my belt. My coding expertise is matched by my leadership skills honed in the film industry, managing teams to meet tight deadlines. I am continuously challenging my comfort zone in coding, with an unwavering goal to make the internet a more inclusive space for all.
Away from my desk, I'm almost always doing something active, whether it be team sports or just getting out for a hike. I'm an avid traveller with a bucket list that doesn't stop growing, and have been to nearly 40 countries so far. I appreciate the balance my hobbies bring to my tech driven life and how they give me the perspective and drive to grow as a developer.
